title: "Alex Frankell receives a Wellcome Career Development Award"
date: 2025-05-01

Alex Frankell has been awarded a [Wellcome](https://wellcome.org/) Career Development Award to support the lab's programme on the longitudinal, non-invasive monitoring of cancer evolution.

Read more on the [ECI website](https://www.earlycancer.cam.ac.uk/news/alex-frankell-receives-wellcome-career-development-award).
